How To Play

How to set up:

Press the reset button.

How to play:

The first person has to spam the letter ‘s’, and the second person has to spam backspace. The first person’s goal is to fill the whole box with letters and the second person has to try to clear the whole box. Player 1 is the person pressing 's' and Player 2 is the person pressing backspace.

How to win:

Fill or clear the whole box. The text below tells which player wins.
